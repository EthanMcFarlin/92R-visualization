COVID-19 State Data Transparency

Interactive Index Mapper

A) View Research Paper

The visualizations in this interactive webapp serve as accompaniment to a larger study conducted by Dr. Sarah James at Harvard University, entitled, "Disease, Data, and Dashboards: Explaining variation in COVID-19 Data Collection and Analysis". Leveraging data from a series of State Public Health Department websites, the paper introduces 6 novel indices, whose values can be downloaded directly or visualized using this tool.
